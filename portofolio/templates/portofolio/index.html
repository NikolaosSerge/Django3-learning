Better everyday!

A simple motto to live by! After all, progress is made one step at a time. Simplify break down revise, take another step and learn at every oppurtunity you get, your pain today will be your strength tomorow. Do your best and all the good you can! In the end we are what we repeatedly do. Go to bed with dreams and wake up with purpose. Take your chances, look alive, oppurtunities do not happen, you create them, a one thing at a time... Be aware of yourself attitudes are contagious; make yours worth catching. Have courage and be kind. Greatness is measured by courage and heart. Everything covetable lies on the other side of fear. Be better everyday and tend to that ideal of perfection...
